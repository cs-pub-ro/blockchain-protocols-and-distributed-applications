üìö How Proof of Stake Works

Proof of Stake (PoS) is a consensus mechanism where validators are chosen to create new blocks based on the amount of cryptocurrency they "stake" (lock up) as collateral.

  • Staking: Validators lock up their cryptocurrency as a stake to participate in consensus
  • Selection: The probability of being chosen to validate is proportional to the stake amount
  • Validation: Selected validators create and validate new blocks
  • Rewards: Validators earn rewards for successfully validating blocks
  • Slashing: Validators can lose their stake if they act maliciously

Advantages over Proof of Work: More energy efficient, faster finality, lower barriers to participation, and built-in economic incentives for security.
