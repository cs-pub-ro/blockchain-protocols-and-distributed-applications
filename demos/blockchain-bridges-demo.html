What is a Blockchain Bridge?

A blockchain bridge is a protocol that connects two separate blockchains, enabling the transfer of assets and data between them. Bridges allow users to:

  • Move assets from one chain to another (e.g., ETH from Ethereum to Polygon)
  • Access DeFi protocols on different chains
  • Reduce transaction costs by using faster/cheaper chains

How it works: When bridging from Ethereum to Polygon, your ETH is locked in a smart contract on Ethereum, and an equivalent amount of WETH is minted on Polygon. To return, you burn the WETH on Polygon and unlock your ETH on Ethereum.
